Experience Tanzania, a magical wildlife safari like no other. Escape deep into the remote untouched wild on an exciting and intriguing excursion, created by the fascinating omnipresent balance between the assorted wildlife, stunning landscape, friendly people, and rich culture.

 

From the Ngorongoro Crater, to the Serengeti national park, and on to the natural wonders of Ruaha national park and Zanzibar beach hideaway, Tanzania is loaded with awe-inspiring tours that tickle the senses. From the snow peaked Mount Kilimanjaro all the way to the wildlife-rich Ngorongoro Crater, the stunning topography will carry to life an unsurpassed Tanzania safari experience. From the northern circuit premier national parks, to the south, west and central parks, the endless exquisiteness will touch even the hardest of hearts.


Home to 14 National Parks and several game reserves, Tanzania safari tours are a grand choice. Not only will they afford visitors front-row seats to one of the wonders of the world, the Wildebeest Migration, Tanzania safari packages will offer a just the once in a lifetime chance to witness the Big Five in their natural surroundings.


Tanzania safari packages commence on arrival to destinations of your choice. World-class luxurious accommodations, in comfortable and elegantly designed lodges, or tented camps, fitted with sufficient modern amenities, are mix together in with nature, in the specially selected remote locations. Every day spent on the safari manifests into a series of surprising, spontaneous and unforgettable natural incidents.


Climbing high to the snow-capped peak of the tallest mountain in Africa Mount Kilimanjaro, or scaling the shorter Mount Meru, is a worthy accomplishment.


Tanzania tour package from India incorporates an assortment of safari options. Operators offer plentiful possibilities on tailor-made adventures, be it hiking, by vehicle, by air balloons or on horseback. Selection of accommodation is either on selected tented camps, or luxurious lodges.


The Ngorongoro Conservation area is a Unesco World Heritage spot that is situated between Manyara and Serengeti national parks. Plateaus, lakes, savannah, volcanoes, and the Ngorongoro Crater, lie within this conservation. Ngorongoro Crater, measures 20km and is 610 meters deep. It is the biggest extinct, intact and unfilled caldera in the world. A descent within the crater’s bowel on a 4x4, offers the opportunity of viewing the 25,000 different animals, including the Big Five. Flamingos, pelicans and hippos, can be sighted on the shores of Lake Magadi.


Tarangire National Park is famous for its huge elephant groups that flood the park and their migratory movement within the park’s ecological unit. Home to a variety of wildlife, large herds of diverse animals can be spotted in the dry seasons of June and October when they converge to drink at the shrinking Tarangire River. Albeit it’s dry, hot and dusty nature, Tarangire has an assortment of rare antelope and gazelle species.


Lake Manyara national park is a microcosm of natural wildlife and landscape. Located at the foot of the Great Rift Valley escarpment, this stunning park is well-known for its tree mountaineering lions. A track within the forest unravels a series of antelopes, primates, and an array of bird species, like the giant silver cheek hornbill. The lake occupies two thirds of the park area and is host to quality bird fauna. Thousands of flamingos seasonally visit the lake.


Selous game reserve is a widespread wilderness in the south of Tanzania. An assortment of wild creatures occupies this reserve, together with the endangered wild dog specie. The Rufiji River has a large hippo and crocodile presence.


Ruaha national park a rugged stretch of wilderness is the largest park in Tanzania, and habitat to the grassland cheetahs, and resident birds like, lovebird, red-billed hornbill, and black collard. River Ruaha meanders throughout the park to provide the residents with water, in this arid expanse. Wild dogs, lions, leopards, cheetahs, spotted and striped hyenas and varieties of hoofed animals live inside the park.


Gombe Stream and Mahale national parks are the world’s most favourite places for chimpanzee tracking. While Gombe Stream is straightforwardly accessible, Mahale is a holistic and demure wilderness for the purist and romantics, who yearn to visit an untamed wild. Located in the western part of Tanzania, only a few people dare to visit these remote pieces of paradise.


Katavi national park is a classic, undisturbed, dry weather park that teems with wildlife. This off the track park is not simply accessible. It has exceptional antelope species like roan and sable, a large number of hippopotamus that converge within the pods, and a mixture of other common wildlife.


The thrilling Saadani National Park opens up to the Indian Ocean’s sandy beaches. Activities range from game drives, sand picnics, motorized boat rides, guided bird watching, a trip to the Maasai community village, and an outing within the only existing wildlife sanctuary, in the coast of East Africa. The beach is also home to local fishermen.


Serengeti safari in Serengeti national park is prominent for its considerable size of lions, this park is located in the north of Tanzania, and extends into Kenya on the south–west. 70 large mammals, including the Big Five, and over 500 avifauna species and birds, live inside the park. This park has the leading number of resident predators: Leopards, Lions, Jackals, Hyenas and Servals. Beautiful birds such as eagles, ostriches, fish eagles and secretary birds reside within the park. The ecological unit is inclusive of rivers, outcropping of rocks called kopjes, termite mounds and savannah plains.


The highlight of the Serengeti safari is the iconic wildebeest and zebra migration, the most unique spectacle in the entire wild world. Commencing within the Ngorongoro plains in the southern part of the Serengeti expanse, this natural phenomenal is a synchronized occurrence that is determined by availability of pasture.


Tanzania luxury safari is all about the astounding prey and predator interaction. Exquisite, comfortably furnished and amenity incorporated retreats from mobile camps to lodges and tented camps, abound within the parks.

The extravagant wildlife safari drives have options of dawn, afternoon, evening or night time, in 4x4 vehicles. Crossing over a variety of flora and fauna, from dusty to dry, swampy and green plus breathing in the fresh crisp air and taking in the animal scents, while merging in with nature is an opportunity one cannot afford to miss.


Endless activities on a tight itinerary range from game tracking walks to night time drives, which provide a matchless view of the nocturnal animals under the starry sky.
