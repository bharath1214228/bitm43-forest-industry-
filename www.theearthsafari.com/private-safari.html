Explore the wilderness at your own pace and privacy.

By choosing The Earth Safari private safaris, you have the luxury of setting your own agenda (with our help) and you won’t be shuffled through the rigid itineraries and fixed timetables which group tours are require to adhere to. You may depart on any day of your choice and for any duration of your choosing. All of our safaris are built around your schedule and not ours. We understand that you demand flexibility and our goal is to provide unlimited choices to meet the requirements of your busy life.

Once on a private safari safari, you have the flexibility to choose and adjust the time you leave and return from your game drives.

Our Private safaris will be exclusive use of your own vehicle and guide.

This type of trip has great advantages for those who enjoy independent travel, are serious photographers, or taking children along.
